# Victory Conditions

Achieving victory on this server is a community effort that will require the participation of every single player on the server. Compared to vanilla gameplay, the endgame recipes were made massively more difficult and extended to include goods from all professions, and additional requirements for victory were added. To destroy the meteor, you must fulfill the following requirements:

1. The Office of the Prime Minister must craft one Computer Lab.

2. The Federal Government must craft 20 Lasers, one of which must be placed on each of the 20 Laser Towers (Dimensions: Minimum bounding box of 5x5x15 | Materials: T4 or T5) that must be constructed centrally on their districts marked on the map by the lowest applicable local authority and connected to the same global electricity grid that all governments must collaborate to provide at least 250 KW of electricity to.

3. The Federation must reach a "Culture Including Children" value of at least 2500.

4. The Federation must have repaid any debt carried over from previous cycles.

## Laser Recipe

The laser recipe has been broken down into multiple components. A detailed recipe is provided to the Federal Government.

Total components required: 80 Balanced Meals, 60 Precision Tools, 60 Enhanced Meals, 20 Special Alloys, 40 Laser Blueprints, 40 Premium Meals, 20 Laser Bodies, 20 Enhanced Precision Tools, 20 Exquisite Meals, 20 Laser Internals, and 20 Final Assembly Instructions.

## Weekly Goals

In an effort to spread out the victory requirements, weekly goals have been established and are laid out in the [[White Tiger] Victory Goal List](https://www.google.com/url?q=https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/u/0/d/1W4JLOZsCzMWtZfd4buRwmc0TKiwOqU4ST8jvJvMn1xE/&sa=D&source=editors&ust=1776691475488683&usg=AOvVaw3TYA2ur5TVwHCmtRamTb2V). The required tasks are outlined below as well as in the checklist, and these checkpoints should be made each week.

Note: Weekly laser crafting goals represent the minimum required progress. Governments are expected to continue supporting the laser craft chain throughout the entire cycle. Completing the minimum does not end the Meteor Store responsibilities for the week; stores, buy orders, and production support should remain active so that later governments are not placed under disproportionate pressure and money actively circulates in the economy.  

### Week 1

* Complete 5% of the Laser Craft
* Hold at least 1 event
* Establish at least one Federal Port
* Connect at least two towns (or states) via the federal highway
* Post at least one update for each ministry in the Newspaper on Discord

### Week 2

* Complete 20% of the Laser Craft
* Hold at least 1 event
* Construct at least 2 Laser Tower
* Federal highway is traversable by vehicle (except bridges)
* Post at least one update for each ministry in the Newspaper on Discord

### Week 3

* Complete 50% of the Laser Craft
* Have at least 1000 Culture, including Children
* Hold at least 1 event
* Complete the global electric grid
* Complete at least 10 Laser Towers
* Post at least one update for each ministry in the Newspaper on Discord

### 

### Week 4

* Complete the Laser Crafts and Computer Lab
* Have at least 2500 Culture, including Children
* Complete all 20 Laser Towers
* Federal Highway is mostly complete, with all towns connected
* Post at least one update for each ministry in the newspaper on Discord
* Host a Meteor Shoot-down Event\*

\*After these conditions are fulfilled, a Game Master is to be informed, and the meteor will be shot in an announced public victory celebration on the Presidential Square or the Presidential Palace. The Prime Minister may choose who "will press the button".

While the Game Masters will be watching for major events and required goals, additional goals and bonuses are available, but must be self-reported on Discord to ensure credit is recorded and counted. Please see the #goal-reports channel and follow the instructions in that channel.

Failure to complete required goals can result in strikes that carry negative consequences. [Suspended for the playtest]

## Special Victories

Additionally, you have the option to strive for bonus rewards that affect the current cycle as well as next cycle rewards by completing goals or tasks aimed at additional victories. A list of goals or tasks can be found in the [[White Tiger] Victory Goal List](https://www.google.com/url?q=https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/u/0/d/1W4JLOZsCzMWtZfd4buRwmc0TKiwOqU4ST8jvJvMn1xE/&sa=D&source=editors&ust=1776691475498767&usg=AOvVaw3trhyjtBUbrCOz5_-XyW7m). You are not limited to these - if you have an idea for a build, event, task, goal, endeavor, quest, scenario, activity, etc - please submit a GM ticket for inclusion and point assignment.

Each task or goal attributes contribution points to a victory type. Once the server acquires enough points to cross a milestone, the reward for that milestone will be granted by the next restart.

To complete an additional victory, the government must complete the basic required victory conditions, acquire enough points to complete the additional victory type, and complete two development tasks:

1. All Federal Government buildings are fully developed to a similar fit, form, and function as their real-life counterparts.
2. Federal Highway is complete.

The same victory type cannot be repeated in two consecutive cycles. They come with in-cycle small bonuses at the quarter milestones (25%, 50%, and 75% completion) and small rewards for the following cycle.

### Domination Victory:

Domination victory is represented by good governance and generally points are awarded for full government participation, faithfully abiding and executing the laws and constitution, addressing the needs of citizens. The federal, state, and local governments in addition to the citizens can contribute points towards this victory.

Reward Theme: Efficiency

Next Cycle Reward: The current Prime Minister may choose to become Federal President and to slightly adjust the server concept with Game Masters consent.

In-Cycle Milestone Rewards:

25% Milestone: Craft time is decreased by 5%

50% Milestone: Gain one additional Settlement Claim Papers per citizen of settlement

75% Milestone: Craft Resources is decreased by 5%

### Religious Victory:

Religious victory is represented by faith and generally rewards points for founding religions, converting citizens, building holy sites, temples, and shrines, and engaging in light RP for religious ceremonies or events. Server milestones and individuals contribute points towards this victory.

Reward Theme: Food

Next Cycle Reward: The religion with the most followers at the end of the cycle becomes the state religion with a tax-funded government office to facilitate endeavors typical for that religion. Players spawn with 20 wheat seeds, 20 huckleberry seeds, 5 bread, and double fish.

In-Cycle Milestone Rewards:

25% Milestone: Nutritional Requirements to complete professions lowered by 2

50% Milestone: Shelf Life of spoilable items increased by 10%

75% Milestone: Dominant religion may bless one food item

### Science Victory:

Science victory is represented by technological advancement, research, and development. Points are generally awarded by completing scientific builds, power-related infrastructure, proliferation of advanced technical skills, and duplicate copies of scientific research books. Federal, State, and Local governments, as well as individuals and server milestones, contribute towards this victory.

Reward Theme: Research

Next Cycle Reward: Players spawn with a full set of iron tools, a soil sampler, 20 charred sausages, 20 topped porridge, and a selection of seeds. The Skill Books for Advanced Baking, Advanced Cooking, Advanced Masonry, Composites, Electronics, Industry, and Oil Drilling cost 50% less to craft.

In Cycle Milestones:

25% Milestone: Advanced Baking, Advanced Cooking, Advanced Masonry, Composites, Electronics, Industry, and Oil Drilling cost 10% less to craft

50% Milestone: Modern Research papers use dynamic inputs for Ink (i.e. affected by modules)

75% Milestone: Modern Research papers use dynamic inputs for Paper (i.e. affected by modules)

### Diplomatic Victory:

Diplomatic victory is represented by cooperation and connected development. Points are generally awarded by having multiple settlements with services and development (such as utilities, roads, garbage disposal, education, etc), hosting diplomatic dinner parties, and founding states. Federal, State, and Local governments and individuals contribute towards this victory.

Reward Theme: Transportation

Next Cycle Reward: The governors of all states fulfilling the conditions are allowed to view the map and get to place their state at a location all of them agree on before the server starts. They are not allowed to share any information about the map.

In Cycle Milestones:

25% Milestone: Asphalt Roads are 10% cheaper

50% Milestone: Run Speed is boosted by 10% on all types of shoes

75% Milestone: Vehicle Movement on roads is increased by 10%

### Culture Victory:

Cultural victory is represented by art and architecture. Points are generally awarded for cultural builds, server milestones regarding Federation culture, hosting or sponsoring events, dinner parties, or activities. Federal, State, and Local governments, as well as individual contributions and server milestones, contribute to this victory.

Reward Theme: Housing

Next Cycle Reward: For the following cycle, the recipes for T5 building materials and furniture are changed to make their costs on par with T4 materials and furniture.

In Cycle Milestones:

25% Milestone: A grant of 200 culture points divided among multiple properties

50% Milestone: Housing requirements to complete professions lowered by 10

75% Milestone: Culture multiplier on housing value increased by 5%
