Labour Requirements

# Nutrition Requirements

You must have a certain amount of subtotal nutrition points to be able to add labour where that requires a specific profession, depending on how advanced it is.

Important: The subtotal nutrition points are not the number displayed in the bottom left of the UI, but the number in the upper section of the tooltip when you hover over it. It is the rolling average of the nutrients you consumed without taking any bonuses from diet balance, preferences or variety into account.

- 50 Subtotal Nutrition Points: Advanced Masonry, Composites, Electronics, Industry and Oil Drilling

- 35 Subtotal Nutrition Points: Advanced Baking, Advanced Cooking, Advanced Smelting, Mechanics and Paper Milling

- 24 Subtotal Nutrition Points: Baking, Basic Engineering, Blacksmith, Carpentry, Cooking, Fertilizers, Glassworking, Masonry, Milling, Painting, Pottery, Shipwright, Smelting and Tailoring

- No Requirement: Butchery, Campfire Cooking, Farming, Gathering, Hunting, Logging, Mining

The brackets were selected based on the four tiers of food skills. 24 subtotal nutrition points can be reached with campfire food, 35 with baking / cooking and 50 with advanced baking / advanced cooking.

For example, pottery is required for baking and smelting for cooking, so they are in the 24 subtotal nutrition points bracket. Advanced smelting is unlocked after pottery and at this point cooking and baking are available, so it requires 35 subtotal nutrition points.

If you for whatever reason get stuck, as you didn't prepare for following these requirements, you can use the command /vomit to clear your stomach and eat correctly to fulfill the requirements. Note that you will only be given this option a single time for your account, after that we will expect that you have read and understood these requirements for the future!

# Housing Requirements

You also must have a certain amount of housing points to be able to add labour where that requires a specific profession, depending on how advanced it is.

- 120 Housing Points: Advanced Masonry, Composites, Electronics, Industry and Oil Drilling

- 60 Housing Points: Advanced Baking, Advanced Cooking, Advanced Smelting, Mechanics and Paper Milling

- 30 Housing Points: Baking, Basic Engineering, Blacksmith, Cooking, Fertilizers, Glassworking, Milling, Painting, Pottery, Shipwright, Smelting and Tailoring.

- No Requirement: Campfire Cooking, Gathering, Hunting, Logging, Mining, Butchery, Farming, Carpentry and Masonry.

# Additional Information

Labour that doesn't require a specific profession does not have any requirements.  
  
These limits ensure that you need to invest into good food and housing, even when you already have all the specializations you wanted to get and would otherwise have no reason to anymore. This improves the economy on the server.
