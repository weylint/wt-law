Companies

# Summary

# If you play with friends and want to do so on the same deed or share any deeds, property (including currency) or workforce you must found a company. You do not need to found a company if you and your friends play on separate deeds and do not share anything nor provide each other with preferential treatment in trade - e.g. treat each other like any other player on the server. In that case, you can stop reading here, as forming a company is then not necessary - but make sure you actually follow the noted requirements, as you otherwise are in violation of our rules and could get convicted by court or punished by Game Masters. If you however form a company to be able to work closer together, be aware that if you and your friends take different skills you may be subjected to a company tax - make sure to read the section on what effects a company has before deciding. There is no way back once a company is founded.

# What is a company?

A company is a legal entity composed of a CEO as founder and several employees joining it. Once founded it cannot be dissolved and its CEO and employees are permanently linked with it.

In a company the CEO and all employees collectively own property through its Legal Person - CEO and employees no longer have any personal property, including personal homesteads. Instead, the company itself has a homestead deed (HQ) with an amount of claimable plots that is based on the number of employees in the company.

Players in a company are not allowed to create or use personally owned bank accounts, including their default personal ones - they must instead use bank accounts owned by their company. They are also not allowed to own any deeds, those must be owned by the Legal Person of the company. Failing to follow this is a violation of § 2 (3) of our server rules.

# Why do companies exist?

Due to how the game currently works, players cooperating closely as a group have an excessive advantage due to their ability to vertically integrate their specialties much more than a solo player can. This allows them to produce the goods they need on their own in a very efficient manner, giving them the ability to sell their products very cheap while at the same time not needing to buy much from other players themselves. This is very damaging to the economy and deemed strongly unfair towards solo players and hence often leads to massive unhappiness with players leaving the server due to feeling uncompetitive and unneeded. On this server we try to be inclusive to everyone though, so we have in countless hours of debate as a community agreed on introducing companies with applied restrictions as a solution to balance groups and solo players. This solution is not set in stone though, if you find a better one reaching the goals, feel free to suggest it.

# Who must found a company?

Every group of at least two players that wishes to share any deeds, property or workforce in the game with each other must found a company. You can only avoid that by not sharing anything with and treating your friends as you would treat any other player on the server, e.g. trade with them on the public market according to the rules of this server. Make sure to make this decision before founding a company, as they can neither be dissolved nor can players leave them once founded and will lead to a tax being applied to the company’s income if you diversify your specialties a lot.

Please note that not founding a company, but still sharing any deeds, property or workforce in the game is a severe violation of § 2 (3) of our server rules.

# How do we found a company?

First, you need to decide on who will be the CEO of the company and manage it.

Important: As employees of a company can’t place a starter camp when their company has a homestead (HQ), it’s not possible for them to retrieve the contents of their starter camps without removing the homestead (HQ) of the company. Hence, make sure that all future employees place their starter camp, retrieve and pick up everything they want and then remove their homestead claim stake again before continuing this process.

Next, you need to decide where you want the homestead (HQ) of the company to be. If the designated CEO already has placed a homestead, this will automatically become the company’s homestead after its creation, otherwise the first homestead placed by an employee of the company after its creation will become the company’s homestead. You can pick up that homestead after the creation of the company and the first homestead placed by any employee will again become the new homestead of the company.

To begin the creation process, the designated CEO needs to type this command:

/company create <Name of Company>

After this, the company will be formed and a citizen called “<Name of Company> Legal Person” will be created that represents your company and determines the citizenship of the company and all its employees. You will automatically be authorised on its bank account “<Name of Company> Company Account” and use this from now on.

Now the CEO needs to invite everyone that should be an employee of the company:

/company invite <Player Name>

The invited players need to accept this invitation, but note that players can only join one company and only when they do not currently have a homestead placed down in the world. The invitation is sent as a notification and can be accepted with this command (which can also be copied from the notification and pasted into the chat):![](https://docs.google.com/docs-images-rt/ABaEjg2TVZao_qkbhy2Sp8_q1N5AVnaStZD9G_rkugGJl5pdSz5Ql1Hfoxie_R7PgXu-D1jMgmn6PiRsuayZ6ZG8CfY7dvQz1CNDDqrirBlfmZsuUmWp8yD9q-uRWmaAD3HmNxbIFcBlR6MJPlt7bcc=s2048)

/company join <Name of Company>

Invites can also be rejected by typing or copying this command:

/company reject <Name of Company>

# How is property of a company managed?

## Joining company

When an invite was accepted, the bank balances of and all deeds owned by the new employee will automatically be transferred to the company’s legal person. The new employee will also be invited to become a resident of the company HQ.

## Creating deeds

New deeds can be created by placing a claimstake, the employee doing so will be able to give it a name in the appearing pop up:

![](https://docs.google.com/docs-images-rt/ABaEjg1yAu9Ghbat6fewqEIPTVD_mfCrLV-aReN7-OX9ySnS6p-C41mRigaZJCC6jwpgQAj8TTo3BnbJYHP0IsLd4n6KXmQ1wlN9IxYSewlUgyAcMqtNATJfzWQrBMBIZLPASX9ppB7OGkAY7MjdK0c=s2048)

It is not necessary to enter a name, in that case it will be automatically generated. The deed created will automatically be transferred to the company's legal person and all members of the company will receive a notification about that:

![](https://docs.google.com/docs-images-rt/ABaEjg37djjyV_jIlBisfRxbT18Ee0hOvaw4TS6FU8gclEeqda1cr4mlgQ2FY1O9ovfOcOKXwLtqxs0V286cXNgJUxLHPzNcm0I10Ce1dZGKroJTUDPsBdwSchqGLTtcW17UCzXQE7a4MJpBm6G-=s2048)

## Editing deeds

Any member of a company can edit any deed owned by the company by selecting their "Claim Tool" and executing the command /company claim, which will set the "Claim Tool" to the selected deed. Note that you must be standing on the deed to claim or unclaim plots.

Betatest as of V12: /company editdeed can be used to edit deeds without a “Claim Tool” - there is no guarantee that this works nor that we keep it as a feature. This is enabled to collect feedback. It is only possible to edit a deed while standing on it.

## Renting out deeds

The command /company rent shows a list of all deeds that can be rented out, selecting a deed will open the rent settings. The company HQ is not listed, as it cannot have residents that aren't members of the company.

## Placing vehicles

The ownership of all vehicles placed by a company member will automatically be transferred to the company's legal person with all company members gaining authorization on the vehicle. Recovering vehicles using the /car-mycars command is supported and can be done by any employee.

## Chatting internally

When a company is founded, a chat channel with a random name is created for it. It will be added as a new tab with the company name as title and is only accessible by employees of the company. It is not possible to manage this channel.

## Creating markers (Betatest as of V12)

The CEO of a company can create “Company Markers” visible for employees by creating a new marker and setting the group to “Company”. To propagate a marker to all employees the command /company updatemarkers must be executed afterwards. This is limited to the CEO for now and will only report errors not success. Changes are possible, feedback appreciated!

This has a side effect for employees: If they use a category named “Company” this will be overwritten by /company updatemarkers.

# Can a company, respectively its employees, join a settlement?

Yes, the citizenship of the company determines the citizenship of all its employees. You can use the following commands to control the citizenship of your company:

Apply for citizenship:         

/company citizenship apply, <NAME OF THE SETTLEMENT>

Join a settlement:

/company citizenship join, <NAME OF THE SETTLEMENT>

Leave a settlement:

/company citizenship leave, <NAME OF THE SETTLEMENT>

# Is it possible to gain reputation with the company?

Yes, a company can gain reputation - but it isn’t possible to give reputation to the company’s Legal Person directly, instead the company’s reputation represents the calculated average reputation of its members. To prevent abuse, it is hence not possible to give reputation to a member of one’s own company.

To calculate the reputation of the company, the relative reputation - consisting of negative and positive reputation summed up - of each employee is summed up and divided by the number of members in the company. The “Speaking Well Of Others” bonus is not counted and will be removed from the relative reputation of each employee before the calculations are made.

# What effects does a company have?

Companies as legal entities can specifically be targeted by everything a player could be.

One example of that is our constitutional law that taxes all monetary income received by companies that have more unique specialties between all its employees than its most advanced employee has specialties:

The amount of unique specialities between all employees of a company is variable A.

The amount of specialties of the employee(s) with the most specialties in a company is variable B.

If A is greater than B, for every A more than B an amount of 20% of the company's income is consecutively removed. For companies that only have two members, this only applies once A is at least 3 (including Self Improvement skill). The mathematical formula for that is: Income \* (1 - 0.80 ^ (A - B))

(When we talk about skills or specialties we mean the game features that you unlock by spending a "star", e.g. Mining, Smelting, Advanced Smelting, etc.)

On a graph that looks as follows, you can see the taxed income and remaining income based on the number of unique skills in your company that the company has in excess to what the most advanced employee in the company has (and as such an equally advanced solo player could have).

![](https://docs.google.com/docs-images-rt/ABaEjg1DJjGVZnKPSohL5lYoDCvDOb7t6ICIRrccor1LU6RZK4SczdkcxvKR3jBoZ9ZtjnHKu2_8szEm2yvTio503gk12zxJItO3WxZZWgjx5yt4hIuczBvG2Yngnm6xLEJjrQ3EIG3igu8BrHqo=s2048)

As you can see it is very important that you communicate with your friends about learning specialties before doing so if you want to avoid the company tax. You can only reduce the tax rate by abandoning excess specialties via the respective game feature.

Tip: If you want to avoid this tax completely, ensure that every employee learns the same specialties - this allows you to play together collaboratively, but not incur the tax.

# What about inactive players in the company?

As already noted, players cannot leave a company. The only exception is if an employee has not played on the server for at least 72 hours and is not going to join the server anymore. In this case, the CEO is allowed to remove the employee from their company. You can do so by using the command: /company fire <PLAYER NAME>.

If not an employee but the CEO fulfils these requirements, you can request the CEO to be replaced by an employee of the company via opening a [ticket on Discord](https://www.google.com/url?q=https://discord.com/channels/254025510651297802/1218289251800711178&sa=D&source=editors&ust=1776708511622735&usg=AOvVaw2cLEspZlnINO28cXtO8N2p). When the CEO is replaced it becomes a normal employee and the prior paragraph applies.

Please note that a player being fired from a company for it to gain a lower company tax rate while still actively playing on the server is a violation of § 2 (1) of our server rules.

# Something doesn’t work correctly, what can I do?

If you experience issues with your company, these debug commands may help:

Fix issues with employee citizenship:

/company citizenship refresh

Fix issues with not having a company HQ:

/company hq checkhqdesync

Fix issues with claim limit of company HQ being incorrect:

/company hq refreshsize

If none of these commands could solve your problem, open a [ticket on Discord](https://www.google.com/url?q=https://discord.com/channels/254025510651297802/1218289251800711178&sa=D&source=editors&ust=1776708511623740&usg=AOvVaw2ostIMQKPqUhAjc5ocU4BR).

# What else should we know?

1. You can find an overview of your company by scrolling below this last paragraph, finding your company in the list and hovering over it.  
2. If you want to know the company tax rate currently applied to your company, type “[Company affected by Company Tax]” into the chat, hover over it and in the tooltip appearing, search for your company and look at the “companyTaxRate”.

3. If you want a breakdown about taxes your company has paid, type /tax othercard <COMPANY NAME> Legal Person into the chat.
