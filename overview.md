# Welcome to White Tiger!

This server is customized to provide the best possible community experience to players, taking into account the high player amount on this server and adjusting recipes for it.

We have both changed vanilla recipes as well as custom new recipes - checking through the categories will help you to understand these changes.

Please note that White Tiger is used as an unofficial playground for testing balance changes before they go into the vanilla game.

White Tiger's motto being "Trial and Error", things get adjusted and change quite often - keeping an eye on these pages may help you a lot with your planning.

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding these changes, you can send me a DM on Discord.

## Financial

### Currencies

An overview of the currencies commonly used on White Tiger:

Euro - Primary trade currency, you must use the Euro to conduct trade, in repair bounties, in work parties, and contracts.

Donation Receipts - A special currency to allow governmental donations. Must be authorized by the Federal President and is not present in every cycle.

Emission Permits - A tracking currency issued to regulate polluting activities. Emission permits control how much you are allowed to pollute in order to keep an ecological balance.

Stat (Tracking) - This is a tracking currency to track multiple stats used in end-game achievements and for other purposes such as taxation, evaluation, analysis, or limitation.

Credit (Tracking) - This is a tracking currency used to track how much credit you have earned. Credit is based on your time on the server as well as a portion of your income. This is part of the loan system explained more in depth below.

Debt (Tracking) - This is a tracking currency used to track how much debt you have acquired. It is received when you trade in Credit (Tracking) for Euros and measures how much you owe.

Personal Credits - Personal credits are not used for trade purposes on this server, however personal credits may be used for various tracking purposes.

Tax Receipts - currency associated with the Income Tax law that is routinely used on White Tiger. Provides a receipt when taxes are paid in advance. Tax overages are refunded to you automatically.

Two Day Tax Projection - currency associated with the Income Tax law that is routinely used on White Tiger. A simple two day tax projection that utilizes your current tax payment multiplied by 48 hours. It is only a projected estimate and assumes that your income/expenses do not change and cannot indicate trend information.

### Loans

There is a federal loan program to provide financial assistance when you might need it. There’s a currency exchange in Unica on the Admin Cave marked with a Federal Loan global marker. Utilize the currency exchange to turn in Credit (Tracking) for Euro. To pay back the loan, utilize the same currency exchange to turn in Debt (Tracking) for Credit (Tracking).

TIP: The Currency Exchange UI can be confusing. To take a loan - utilize the Credit & Euro option. To pay a loan - utilize the Debt & Credit Option.

Loan Details:

Loans are constructed of two phases: Borrowing Phase and Repayment Phase.

Borrowing Phase:

Borrow up to your Credit (Tracking) limit. Your credit becomes frozen once you take a loan until you pay it back. If you don’t borrow your total Credit (Tracking) limit, you can take additional loans up to that limit.

The Borrowing Phase lasts for 7 days and starts when you first take a loan. During this phase, you will accrue interest and make interest payments of 0.1% of the Debt (Tracking) amount (automatically) every hour. This amounts to an interest rate of 2.4% per day.

Repayment Phase:

Once the initial 7 days have passed, the loan will transition into the Repayment Phase. During this phase, the loan will automatically collect both interest and principal payments amortized over a 14-day window. Once this repayment has concluded, you are able to take an additional loan.

Manual repayment is always possible at the currency exchange in Unica. Once a loan has been fully repaid, you will receive a bump in Credit (Tracking) as your credit will be unfrozen and you will be able to take a new loan as needed.

As interest is charged per hour, it is advised to pay back the loan as soon as possible.

### Taxes

Taxes are necessary on White Tiger and pay for various amenities and necessities such as research, infrastructure, and meteor defense. There are different types of tax systems that may be applied by the Federal Government and it’s not possible to list all here.

Generally, you may see sales taxes from towns or states and income based taxes from the Federal Government.

The command /tax card can show lots of important information related to your taxes. Likewise, the commands /tax othercard can show information regarding other player’s accounts. And /tax govcard can give additional information about government accounts.

The government typically gives additional information in a !tax command.

There are constitutional limits on taxation. You can review those in the Constitution document.

The most commonly used federal tax on White Tiger is the Income Tax. It utilizes your income and expense stats over the last 48 hours (rolling period) to determine your taxable income, tax bracket, and tax rate via a progressive percentage and charges an hourly tax. The specific details of the law are chosen by Parliament and may vary from cycle to cycle or even week to week. Look out for Newspaper posts for changes in taxation.

## Social Changes

White Tiger is made up of many players and different playstyles. The players shape the server through feedback, governmental policy, or referendums.

If there’s something that you don’t like or wish you could change - get involved!

You can run for office, join as a minister in government, or offer help as a civil servant if you just want to focus on a small task.

If the government has made changes that you think are unconstitutional or contribute to a negative experience - you can contact the government, make a request, leave feedback for them. Please be respectful and explain why you find the particular policy challenging or unhelpful.

If the government is not meaningfully listening to your concerns - you have options! You can file a court case if you believe the action or law is unconstitutional. You can also start a referendum to modify or repeal the law - this will put it to a citizen’s vote. A simple majority is sufficient to enact change, but if enough people agree it can become constitutional.

### Court

There are two levels: Magistrate Court, which handles violations of federal law and disputes between players, and the Supreme Court, which handles appeals and constitutional challenges.

Magistrate Judges are regular community members. Anyone in good standing with a genuine interest in the role can apply, and once appointed it is a paid position held indefinitely with no term limit or election cycle. Judges can take on cases at their own discretion for as long as they remain active. The Supreme Court Justice is Dennis, the Eco Community Manager.

To open a case, start a thread in the Federal Court channel. Note that anyone with knowledge of a law violation can open a case - it doesn't have to be the direct victim.

Roleplay is completely optional. You can treat a court case exactly like a ticket: submit your evidence, answer any relevant questions, and be done with it. If you don't want to engage in roleplay with the other party, just say so and the case proceeds without it. Private court threads are also available if you'd prefer to keep things out of the public channel.

If you're the defendant, you're never forced to participate — but it's strongly advisable to put forward a defense or acknowledgment, since a verdict can be reached on hard evidence alone. The outcome of a court case is very rarely an administrative penalty, which is another reason to engage rather than ignore it.

You can also hire a lawyer, another player who acts on your behalf, if you'd prefer not to handle the case yourself.

Note: Law violations reported via GM ticket will be forwarded to the court.

### Referendums

If there's a law you'd like to see added, changed, or removed, you can put it directly to a citizen vote through a referendum. Referendums can propose specific law changes (additions, modifications, or repeals) or ask a simple yes/no question to the population.

To submit a referendum, draft your proposed law change or question and send it to Dennis via Discord DM.

From there, it goes to Parliament for review. Parliament will share their advice and reasoning with the community, but the final decision always rests with the citizens. Parliament cannot block or override a vote.

Once Parliament has weighed in, the referendum opens for a vote that every citizen can participate in.

For a referendum to pass, it needs:

* At least 2.5% of the population to vote
* The server to have at least 10% of the population active at the time

For a referendum to affect the Constitution, the bar is higher:

* At least 67% support
* A turnout of at least 25% of the active population

## General and Skill Changes

This page gives an overview of specific changes on White Tiger that are different from Vanilla.

### General

* The federation is a starting government with currency (Euro). All trades must be in Euro. Other currencies exist but are used for other purposes, more information can be found in F1 Ecopedia section.
* Endgame recipes (i.e. Lasers) are significantly harder than vanilla and intended to require an effort by every single player on this server.
* Research is centralized by the Federal University.

* Module recipes have been modified to work with the Education release schedule
* Research papers are crafted on the research table or laboratory and will generate garbage or compost
* Research papers are not affected by modules

* Easier Settlement Setup

* All settlement tables have been removed from their professions and made generic at the settlement crafting table to empower earlier town government setups
* Countries require 1 town, 15 players, and 60 culture.

* Quality of Life Changes

* Parts durability should last approximately 25 hours
* Power generation and fuel capacity for Combustion Generator and Industrial Generator modified
* Balanced vehicle weight capacity and storage (affecting canoes, wheelbarrow, and carts)
* Starting inventory includes a fishing pole and small canoe
* Early wild food gathering boost diminishes over the first 3 days (250%, 200%, 150%)
* The Outpost Table is used as a general Marketplace table to have a central table for the blueprints

* Housing and Rooms

* Holes typically used for windows or doors are not permitted for a room to be valid. Ladder or some hewn shapes can be used for easy store windows.
* Multiple Residents (hotels, rentals, friends, etc) Housing points vary based on occupancy as follows: 1 = 100 %; 2 = 112,5 %, 3 = 125 %; 4 = 112,5 %; 5 = 100 %; 6 = 90 %; 7 = 80 %; 8 = 70 %, 9 = 60 %, 10 or more = 50 %.

### Hunting

* Fishing limitations: Fish Traps / Crab Pots have minimum distances to prevent industrial level farms
* Fishing limitations: Fishery has reduced storage visibility

### Farming

* Ethanol recipes are moved to farming from Cutting Edge Cooking

### Campfire Cooking

* Added new recipe: Porridge (new food item consumes seeds)

### Oil Drilling

* Oil yield depletion based on consumption

### Cutting Edge Cooking

* Skill is removed and recipes moved to farming

## Useful Commands

### Tax Commands

/tax card - used to see any tax, rebate, payment, or suspended tax charged to you

/tax othercard - used to see any tax, rebate, payment, or suspended tax charged to another player

/tax govcard - used to see any tax, rebate, payment, or suspended tax owed to the government

!tax - used as part of the government tax laws to send you relevant information pertaining to your taxes

### Server / Utility Commands

!laserprogress - sends information about laser components that have been crafted compared to the total required

/time now - shows the current server time. Useful for checking education schedule

/unstuck - useful if you’re stuck to send you vertically to the nearest surface. Can sometimes work horizontally if near edges/corners.

/mark - can drop a marker at your location, accepts an optional string, e.g. /mark house

/ignore <player> - adds or removes a player to or from your ignore list.

### Food or Nutrition Commands

/vomit - used to reset your stomach contents. This is only usable once per lifetime.

/mm taste - shows what your character likes to eat. Useful for dinner parties.

### Room Commands

/rooms test - test for room condition issues and can update outdoor scoring

/rooms diagnose - provides detailed diagnostic information why a room is failing.

### Vehicle Commands

/car-mycars - used to summon your vehicles. Gives a copyable command to summon to you or to send to void storage in case your vehicle is otherwise unrecoverable. Do not use this command to freely transport goods for buying or selling - abuse of the command can result in access to it being disabled.

/freecar - minor car movement command allows you to replace the vehicle with your hammer up to 6 meters away.
